Who Am I?

I am a Bangladeshi student, founder of ariyanriad.blogspot.com, and official ambassador for 5 international competitions: International Astronomy and Astrophysics Competition (IAAC), International Computer Science Competition (ICSC), International Youth Math Challenge (IYMC), International Linguistics Challenge (ILC), and Global Quantum Mechanics Challenge (GQMC).

I'm passionate about making science accessible through content creation, building web tools, and inspiring students worldwide. I've built 8 interactive web tools and written over 100 educational blog posts.
